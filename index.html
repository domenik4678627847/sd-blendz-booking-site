Hours and rules

How this booking flow works

  • Appointments are available Monday to Friday only.
  • Every service now books a full 1-hour time block.
  • Available times are 4-5, 5-6, 6-7, 7-8, and 8-9.
  • The latest available start is 8:00 PM so the last appointment ends at 9:00 PM.
  • Once a time is booked, any overlapping times disappear from the list for that day.
