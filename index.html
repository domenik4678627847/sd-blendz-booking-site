Hours and rules

How this booking flow works

  • Appointments are available Monday to Friday only.
  • Standard services take 30 minutes and longer cuts can take 45 minutes.
  • Times are offered in 30-minute steps, but longer services block overlapping slots.
  • The latest available start adjusts automatically so the service still ends by 8:30 PM.
  • Once a time is booked, any overlapping times disappear from the list for that day.
